How yoga can make your life better

When I moved to the USA in 2014, I lived with an Indian American family, and the mom introduced me to yoga. She told me about the benefits, and I started to practice on my own after watching a series of 30 days Yoga Challenge on Youtube.

Here are the main benefits of daily yoga practice:

  • Helps manage stress and anxiety
  • Keeps you relaxing
  • Improves strength, balance and flexibility
